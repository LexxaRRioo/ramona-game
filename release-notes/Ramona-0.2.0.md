## Fixed
- She now stands right on top of the Dock instead of floating just in front of it - macOS reports the Dock's on-screen area slightly taller than what's actually visible, so she used to settle a few points above the real surface.
- The lie-down-and-curl-up transition into sleep no longer flips between facing left and right mid-animation. She now picks one side and curls consistently through the whole settle.

## Added
- She settles facing the direction she was headed after walking, running, or climbing, instead of always turning to face front.
- Grooming right after waking up plays a lying-down wash instead of sitting up first.
- Being picked up now shows a startled, alert pose instead of freezing on whatever frame she happened to be on.
